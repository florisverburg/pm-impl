How does it work?

@message

Register and logging in

You can register or log in using the button in the top right corner. By registering and logging in, you will be able make use of the system. You can either register and log in using your email address or by logging in using your LinkedIn account.
When logged in, the system gives you the possibility to:
  • View your profile
  • Edit your profile
  • View the practicals which you are enrolled for
  • View a specific practical

Subscribing to a practical

When you are logged in, you can add a practical to your account. This is done by clicking on the URL that your teacher sent you. A teacher that uses our system will be able to retrieve this URL from the system and email this to the participants.

Inviting other students

When you are logged in and subscribed to a practical, you can start forming your group. You can go search and invite other participants who are also currently looking for a group. The system also gives you recommendations on which participants to invite, see below for more information about the recommendations.

Recommendations

To help you quickly find the best group members, we added a recommendation algorithm to the system. This algorithm combines your skills, other peoples skills and the skills filled in by the practical teacher to recommend you other students. The algorithm aims to have a minimal differentiation between a group and the skills filled in by the practical teacher. The teacher's skills are the skills that are recommended for each practical group. Since we only recommend, you can always choose to invite someone else.
