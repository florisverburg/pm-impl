The problem

Since the start of MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses, for more info click here!) their popularity has grown substantially. This created two needs:

  • Practicals and assignments for these courses need to be easily manageable
  • Participants need an easy and reliable way of searching through and inviting their group members

What is the purpose of APMatch?

The purpose of this website is to provide a platform for students to search for group members and invite them to their group. The platform provides for an easy to use invitation system that gives complete control over your invitations.
The platform also provides for a recommendation algorithm. This algorithm can recommend other students to you using data about some recommended skills.
Interested in how it works? Check our How does it work? page.
